Memo_Initial_Codebook_Rationale

The initial codebook was developed after familiarization with the five structured observation transcripts. The framework was designed to remain closely aligned with the observation instrument while also capturing the classroom processes most relevant to explaining the quantitative findings. The codebook is primarily deductive and includes domains related to instructional format, student engagement, peer interaction, metalinguistic use, teacher role, and observed constraints. It remains provisional and may be refined after first-cycle coding.
