Error Categories
Vocabulary Errors (V): Vocabulary errors refer to problems related to word choice and 
lexical use. They include:

Lexical errors.

Wrong word choice.

Literal translation.
Grammar Errors (G): Grammar errors refer to problems related to the grammatical structure 
of sentences. They include:

Tense errors, including incorrect verb tense or tense form.

Subject–verb agreement errors, including singular/plural mismatch.

Errors in articles, pronouns, and prepositions.

Incorrect use of function words.
Mechanics Errors (M): Mechanics errors refer to problems related to the technical 
conventions of writing. They include:

Spelling errors, including misspelled words.

Capitalization errors, including missing or incorrect capitalization.

Punctuation errors, including missing or incorrect punctuation.
